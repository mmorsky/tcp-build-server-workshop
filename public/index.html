More women choose IT than ever before

Universities are getting record number of women applicants in fields traditionally considered men dominated. Number of women applicants have increased at least in technology, mathematics and physics departments. According to a student unions, this has been a record year in increasing number of female students in technological subjects.

Half of company board members are women

Half of all company board members are now women. In recent study made by the Economist, women are rising to the board positions faster than ever. Also changes to the parental benefits have increased the number of women in management positions.

Eating donuts is actually good for you

According to a recent study published in Scientific American eating one donut a day can significantly increase your overall life happiness. Scientists have discovered that having a donut per day decreases your blood pressure and releases happiness hormones to your body.
